Top Potluck Pro-Tips

  • Transport Smart: Use coolers with ice packs for cold dishes. Wrap hot dishes in towels inside an insulated carrier.
  • Label Everything: Bring a small card listing the main ingredients to help guests with allergies or dietary restrictions.
  • Feta Choice: For the best flavor and texture in salads, always buy feta cheese in a block packed in brine, not pre-crumbled.
  • Plan for Temperature: To keep dishes at the right temperature on the buffet, place cold bowls in a larger bowl of ice. Use a slow cooker on "warm" for hot dishes.
